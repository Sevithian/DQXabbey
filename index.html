Clarity Disclaimer

It's come to our attention that some people visiting this site might mistakenly conflate DQXabbey's affiliation with the Clarity project. While some members of DQXabbey contribute to DQXclarity's efforts, there's no affiliation between the two projects. DQXabbey was created solely as a repository of information about the game stemming from guides and resources created by and for the English community.


While the support the abbey has garnered is certainly appreciated, concerns have arisen about credit being given where credit is due. To reiterate, DQXabbey is NOT AFFILIATED WITH DQXCLARITY, and although information pertaining to Clarity is present on the site, we do NOT take credit for the tools, the installation process, or any other information sourced from Clarity's developer, Serany.


So please, if you're interested in the translation utilities for Dragon Quest X, head over to the project's official GitHub repository, where the information about the project is sourced from and support the developer of these tools. Nothing on this site would be possible without him. If you're looking for specific information about the project, interviews, questions and concerns, or otherwise, please make sure you reach out to Serany directly. Although DQXabbey can be useful as a reference point for various parts of the installation process, all information about Clarity should be sought out first and foremost from the developer and the repository.


Once again, please make sure to give a heartful thanks to Serany for all the contributions he's made to the English community. This site, the community, and the ease of access his tools have afforded new players would not and could not exist without him.




Site Welcome

This site is meant to be a repository of information for players who are interested in getting started playing Dragon Quest X Online, as well as people who are interested in the English tools and translation efforts associated with it. The information you'll find on this site is catered to help you learn everything you need to know about the game to not only get started playing, but get comfortable and acquainted with the game and helpful information that'll kickstart your DQX experience.


The information on this site was provided by users of the English Dragon Quest X community, if you see incorrect information or information you'd like added or appended to any part of the site, please feel free to reach out to myself or a community member on Discord. If you'd like to contribute to the site, please consider contacting us through the appropriate channels and we'll be more than happy to consider your addition. Any efforts to provide more, or further clarify, information pertaining to the game or the topics listed on this site are always welcome.


That being said, I hope you'll take the time to use this site as an easy way to approach getting started with this wonderful game. There are many helpful topics listed on each page, and new pertinent information will be added here as we get updated. If you have any questions, or would like to join the ever-expanding English community for the game, please consider checking out the "Community" tab on this site as well.


Hope to see you in game soon! - Sevithian / (@sevithian)
